About MoodMirror

MoodMirror is a gentle space for emotional reflection, inspired by Ubuntu values and the need for culturally relevant mental health support in Kenya.

Our Journey

  • 2023: Concept born from a mental health pitch deck
  • 2024: First prototype built at University of Nairobi
  • 2025: MoodMirror goes live to support emotional wellness

A Note from Praise

I built MoodMirror to be more than a website—it’s a quiet companion for anyone navigating life’s emotional waves. I hope it feels like a safe space, especially for those who rarely find one.
